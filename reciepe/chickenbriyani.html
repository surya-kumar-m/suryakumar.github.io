Chicken Briyani

Ingredients

  1. Rice - 500g
  2. Water - 750ml (Ratio Rice : Water = 2 : 3)
  3. Chicken - 500g
  4. Green chilli - 2(big)
  5. Chilli Powder - 1tbs
  6. Ginger - 50g
  7. Garlic - 25g
  8. Curd - 100ml
  9. Mint - 30 leaves
  10. Coriander - 30 leaves
  11. Onion - 3(medium) (200g)
  12. Tomato - 3(medium) (200g)
  13. Cinnamon - 2 piece
  14. Cloves - 2 piece
  15. Cardamom - 2 piece
  16. Bay Leaf - 2 piece
  17. oil - 100 ml
  18. salt - 1 tbs

Procedure

  • Step 1 : Add oil + onion + cinnamon + cloves + cardamom till onion turns golden brown.
  • Step 2 : Sim flame. Add Coriander + 10 sec mix for every following Ingredients + mint + chilli + ginger garlic + chilli powder + tomato + curd + salt. Cook for 5 mins in medium flame.
  • Step 3 : Sim flame. Add chicken and mix with masala for 1 minute. Increase the flame. Add water close the cooker lid without whistle. Now, wait for smoke to rise from the nostril.
  • Step 4 : Sim flame. Open and stir. Increase the flame. Add rice and stir for 10 sec once started boiling check masala. If tangy required add half lemon. Stir slowly till rice and water reaches the same level. Close and put whistle. Cook for only one whistle and turn off the gas.
