Tea Kadai Bonda

Ingredients

  1. Onion - 3(medium)
  2. Green Chilli - 2(big)
  3. Coriander Leaves - half hand
  4. Curry leaves - 20
  5. Besan flour - 4tbs
  6. Rice flour - 2tbs
  7. Maida - 1tbs
  8. Asafoetida powder(perungayum) - 1/4tbs
  9. Baking soda - 1/4tbs
  10. Chilli powder - 1/3tbs
  11. Salt - 1/3tbs

Procedure

  • Step 1 : Slice onion vertically,chilli,coriander,curry leaves finely.
  • Step 2 : Add all sliced ingredients in a bowl and add chilli powder,asafoetida powder,baking soda and salt and mix throughly without water.
  • Step 3 : Add besan,rice and maida flour and mix it well with the water content in the onion itself.
  • Step 4 : Fry with medium flame.
